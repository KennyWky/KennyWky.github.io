Benefits of Smart City

  A smart city is an urban area that combines modern infrastructure with advanced technology for intelligent management. It efficiently manages resources such as energy, water, and waste by utilizing sensors and networks for real-time monitoring and control. This approach promotes energy conservation, encourages resource recycling, and reduces wastage, leading to environmentally friendly city operations.
  Smart cities also employ diverse technologies to address traffic congestion. Real-time traffic information, smart traffic signal control, and autonomous vehicles optimize traffic flow, reducing accidents and enhancing overall transportation efficiency.
  Residents benefit from a wide range of convenient services provided by smart cities. Smart homes, buildings, and electronic health management systems enable residents to easily and securely control their living environments. Additionally, smart facility reservations and public service applications facilitate efficient management of daily life.
  Sustainable development is a key focus for smart cities. They emphasize energy efficiency, the utilization of renewable energy, and the implementation of eco-friendly architecture, contributing to climate change adaptation and the preservation of valuable resources.
  Additionally, smart cities enhance urban safety through the integration of CCTV, sensor networks, and artificial intelligence technology. By implementing crime prevention and accident detection systems, they ensure the safety of citizens and enable swift responses to emergency situations. Moreover, leveraging big data, smart cities can predict and prevent crimes, allowing for improved city security and efficient deployment of police resources.
  In conclusion, smart cities integrate modern infrastructure and advanced technology to intelligently manage resources, optimize transportation, and enhance residents' convenience. With a strong emphasis on sustainable development, they promote energy efficiency, renewable energy usage, and eco-friendly architecture to create a greener future.

Online Grocery Shopping

  In recent years, online grocery shopping has gained huge popularity way for people to buy food and ingredients. The convenience of searching and purchasing groceries at home has become a big advantage.
  According to a survey conducted by the Korea KREI, the number of households purchasing food ingredients through Online grocery shopping has consistently increased over the years. The proportion of households buying groceries online more than once a month was only 8.2% in 2013 but increased to 56.3% in 2022. The proportion of households responding that their main food shopping location is online was negligible at around 0.1% in 2013. However, after a decade, it has risen significantly to approximately 4% (7% for processed food) in 2022. In 2022, the most important factor considered by households when purchasing groceries online was "price," accounting for 35.4% of the respondents. This suggests that online grocery shopping is gaining popularity among consumers due to its affordability compared to offline options. Due to these reasons,it is expected that the number of consumers purchasing food ingredients online will continue to increase. Due to the growing trend of purchasing food ingredients online, our Smart Recipe app will provide features related to the Rakuten shopping mall. Users will be able to easily search for specific items, just like the suggestions provided by the Smart Recipe app. They can purchase or browse ingredients related to the searched food on the Japanese online marketplace, Rakuten. The integration of recipe recommendations and online shopping is expected to provide users with a seamless experience, enhancing their culinary exploration.
